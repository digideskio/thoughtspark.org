Jeremy Whitlock So you want to know about me huh?

My name is Jeremy Whitlock and I am a lover of God, a husband, a father to two awesome boys and a Tough Mudder. At work, I'm a Senior Software Engineer at Appcelerator, and in my spare time I'm an open source evangelist and contributor. I am a firm believer that knowledge is power and while I may not know everything, I make up for it in a strong drive to learn and better myself.

Open Source Contributions

I've been contributing to open source for over a decade now. It started back in the day with OpenEJB, CruiseControl and Subversion but has now changed to things like jclouds, psutil and TextMate.
